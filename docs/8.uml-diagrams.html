The book, "[The Elements of UML Style](http://bit.ly/se-elements-of-uml-style)", by Scott W. Ambler is a good reference for more - we have pulled many insights from it here. --- class: center, middle name: best-practices # Best Practices --- # Best Practices Regardless of which diagram type, following best practices keeps diagrams understandable and avoids common pitfalls. - Avoid crossed lines - Avoid diagonal and curved lines - Use consistent sizes - Flow from top-to-bottom, left-to-right --- # Best Practices ## Avoid crossed lines Don't do this: ![crossed lines](../images/uml_lines_cross_no_jump.png) --- # Best Practices ## Avoid crossed lines Do this: ![crossed lines](../images/uml_lines_cross_jump.png) --- # Best Practices ## Avoid diagonal and curved lines.
